Native American Athletic Foundation

My work with the Native American Athletic Foundation focuses on visual storytelling for events like the anual football and baseball games and seasonal clinics. Through this role, I create promotional graphics, athlete features, and social media content that help connect players, families, and communities.

In addition to design work, I conduct interviews with athletes, coaches, and program leaders. Using those conversations, I write feature articles and contribute to the foundations monthly newsletter. This has helped me develop strong storytelling skills while learning how to communicate meaningful experiences through written content.

I have also contributed to the design of merchandise, helping create apparel and branded items that reflect the identity of the program while promoting representation and pride within the community.

One of the most impactful experiences was working at the Vikings U.S. Bank Stadium during the Native All-American Game, where I captured photography and contributed to live social media coverage throughout the event.

Being in that environment gave me hands-on experience creating content in a fast-paced, high-energy setting. I focused on capturing real-time moments, highlighting athletes, and helping tell the story of the event through visuals that reflected both competition and culture.

Overall, this role has strengthened my ability to combine design, photography, writing, and storytelling to support representation and visibility for Native athletes.
