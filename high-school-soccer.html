High School Work

My first real experience with sports media started with my high school soccer team. I didn’t just want scores and schedules, I wanted our friends, family, and classmates to feel like they were part of the season. So I created graphics, photos, and social media posts that highlighted players, promoted big games, and captured the moments between the whistles.

Throughout the season, I created spotlights for key players, using action photos and short captions that shared their position, year, and fun facts. These posts helped build team identity and gave players something personal to repost and share.

This project showed me how powerful consistent, thoughtful content can be for even a small team, and it sparked my interest in taking sports media more seriously.
