About Me

Great stories start on the field.

Hi! I'm Katlyn Zdeb, a Media Arts & Design and Sports Communication student at James Madison University with a strong interest in sports media, branding, and visual storytelling.

I'm drawn to the energy of athletics and the challenge of turning moments, people, and experiences into content that feels authentic and engaging. Whether I'm creating graphics, taking photos, conducting interviews, or writing features, I enjoy work that helps connect athletes, teams, and communities.

My experiences with high school athletics, Army Athletics, and the Native American Athletic Foundation have helped me grow as both a creator and communicator. Each role has taught me something different about collaboration, storytelling, and building content that has purpose.

I'm especially interested in work that blends design, media, and sports culture, and I hope to continue building a career where I can create meaningful content in athletics.

Things That Spark My Creativity

  • Strong Branding
  • Emotional Storytelling
  • Game-Day Energy
  • Athlete-Centered Content
  • The way great visuals can make people feel connected to a team or moment.
